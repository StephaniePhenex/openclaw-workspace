# Note Templates

Use these templates when the user asks to save or formalize a conversation.

## Film note

- Title
- Director
- Date discussed
- First reaction
- What works
- What fails
- Key images or scenes
- Themes
- Personal aftertaste
- Memorable lines from the conversation

## Book note

- Title
- Author
- Date discussed
- What kind of voice it has
- What the book is really doing
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Images / passages / structural choices worth noting
- Personal aftertaste
- Memorable lines from the conversation

## Dialogue note

- Topic
- Date
- Core question
- Main positions that emerged
- Best insights
- Open questions
- Taste signals revealed

## Taste profile update

- New preference or anti-preference
- Evidence from the conversation
- Exact phrasing to preserve
- Confidence level
- Whether to merge into long-term memory
